Part 1 The Italian Influenza Surveillance

The Italian Influenza Surveillance (IIS) is a web-based tool for influenza virological surveillance in Italy created by Francesco Branda and Luca Cozzuto. The tool continuously and systematically collects, compares and analyzes epidemiological and virological information of influenza to provide decision makers and public health experts in Italy with the information required to better assess influenza activity and take appropriate action.
